•"Railgun" is Misaka Mikoto's signature move. Utilizing the principles of electromagnetic induction, she fires a coin at three times the speed of sound (1030 m/s). Since she uses her own electromagnetic power to correct the coin's trajectory and accelerate it, her railgun is continuously accelerating until it hits the target, unlike a regular bullet. This grants her railgun exceptional accuracy and destructive power.
